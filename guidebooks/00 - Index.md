# Selling Post Guidebook

> A comprehensive reference for writing high-converting selling posts for business accounts on social media.

## What Is a Selling Post?

A selling post is **not just an advertisement**. It is a strategic tool for one of three goals:

- **Lead Generation** — attract new potential customers into your orbit
- **Lead Nurture** — build trust and move existing leads closer to a decision
- **Lead Conversion** — prompt a specific buying action

The key mindset shift: stop broadcasting. Start building relationships at scale.

## Quick Reference

### The Content Mix Rules

- **80/20 Rule**: 80% value (education, inspiration) → 20% promotional
- **50/30/20 Rule**: 50% educational → 30% curated industry content → 20% promotional

### The Core Conversion Sequence

```
Hook → Value → Client Story → CTA
```

### The Trust Ladder

```
Unaware → Problem Aware → Solution Aware → Product Aware → Most Aware
```
